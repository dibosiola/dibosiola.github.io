I am a PhD student in Computer Science at Queen Mary University of London. My Supervisors are Gareth Tyson and Steve Uhlig.
My research interests are Internet measurements, pirated content distribution, online privacy and malicious activites. At the moment I am working on pirated video distribution over the Internet.

Prior to commencing my PhD, I gained a MSc (Distinction) in Computer Science at Queen Mary University of London and a BSc (Upper Second Class) in Computer Science at Bayero University, Kano.

Please drop me an email if your're interested in collborating and brainstorming on ideas.
